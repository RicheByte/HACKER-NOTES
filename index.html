HACKER NOTES (hacker.super.site) is a curated terminal-themed blog by RicheByte, designed for IT enthusiasts and cybersecurity learners. Whether you're diving into Linux, exploring networking protocols, or sharpening your ethical hacking skills—this site serves as your command-line companion.

It goes beyond tutorials—it’s a minimalist space for anyone passionate about the digital frontier. Stay sharp with writeups, quick cheatsheets, and notes grounded in real-world cybersecurity learning.

Why? Because the best hackers never stop learning.
